Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Super Live Wallpaper

Effective date: February 8, 2026

Super Live Wallpaper is built to work locally on your Mac. We keep privacy simple: no accounts, no tracking, and no online video downloads.

1. Data Collection

  • We do not collect personal data.
  • We do not track users across apps, websites, or devices.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs.

2. Accounts and Sign-In

  • No account is required.
  • No sign-in is required.

3. File Access and Local Storage

  • The app only accesses files you explicitly select through the macOS file picker.
  • Supported input files are local .mp4 and .mov videos.
  • Imported clips, exports, and thumbnails are stored locally in your app container on your Mac.
  • No files are uploaded to external servers by the app.

4. Network Usage

  • The app does not provide online video downloading.
  • The app does not provide streaming from external video services.
  • Core functionality works offline with local files.

5. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not share personal information with third parties.

6. Your Control

  • You can delete clips from within the app at any time.
  • Deleting clips removes related local files created by the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date on this page.

9. Contact
